# Support

## Asking Questions

For general questions about setup, usage, or how something works, open a [Discussion](../../discussions).

## Reporting Bugs

If you have found something that is not working as expected, open a [bug report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.md).

## Requesting Features

Have an idea? Open a [feature request](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.md).
